Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the Youth Fellowship?

The Youth Fellowship is open to all young people between the ages of 13 and 35. We welcome both members of Gwarinpa Anglican Church and visitors from other churches.

When and where do you meet?

We meet every Sunday at 8:00 AM in the church auditorium for Youth Service. We also have Bible Study on Sundays at 4:00 PM and Prayer Meeting on Wednesdays at 6:00 PM.

Do I need to register to attend events?

For regular meetings, no registration is required. However, for special events, retreats, and outreach programs, registration is usually required. Check our Events page for details.

How can I get involved in serving?

We have various service opportunities including ushering, media team, worship team, outreach committee, and more. Contact any of our leaders or fill out the contact form to express interest.
